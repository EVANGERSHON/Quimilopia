## 🔬 About Quimilopia

Quimilopia is a web-based tool made to simulate real-world labs. It helps you learn chemistry by allowing you to try experiments safely on your computer. The platform gives you access to virtual tools that make learning and practicing chemical studies easier and more interactive. Quimilopia works on any device with a web browser, but this guide focuses on running it on Windows.

The app suits users who want to study atoms, molecules, and chemical reactions without needing physical lab equipment. It provides a space for education, experimentation, and even some chemical engineering basics.

Quimilopia is designed to be reliable, safe, and easy to use. You won’t need programming skills to get started.

## 🌟 Key Features

- Simulate chemical reactions virtually  
- Visualize atoms and molecules in 3D  
- Access a digital periodic table  
- Save and review your experiments  
- User-friendly interface for learners at all levels  
- Secure environment with no real chemicals needed  
- Scalable to support many users simultaneously

Quimilopia runs in your browser and does not send your data to external servers unless you opt-in. Your experiments and data stay on your computer unless you choose to save them to the cloud.

Because it is a virtual lab, you do not handle real chemicals, removing any physical risks related to chemical experiments.
